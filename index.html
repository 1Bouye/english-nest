Common questions

Quick answers

How long is the first lesson?

Your first lesson with Sidi or Mohamed Bouye is 30 minutes — a real lesson to get you started on the right track.

Do I need to take the quiz first?

Not required, but we recommend it. The quiz takes under 5 minutes and helps us match you to the right level from day one.

Can I switch tracks mid-journey?

Absolutely. If your goals change or you want to add a second track, just let us know and we'll adjust your plan.

What languages are classes in?

All lessons are in English. Support notes in Arabic or French can be provided if needed at beginner levels.
