Common questions

Quick answers

How long is the free trial?

Your free trial is a 30-minute lesson with one of our teachers. No credit card, no commitment — just a real lesson.

Do I need to take the quiz first?

Not required, but we recommend it. The quiz takes under 5 minutes and helps us match you to the right teacher and level from day one.

Can I switch tracks mid-journey?

Absolutely. If your goals change or you want to add a second track, just let us know and we'll adjust your plan.

What languages are classes in?

All lessons are in English — that's how you learn fastest. Support notes in Arabic or French can be provided if needed, especially at beginner levels.
